# Concept Taxonomy

The following taxonomy categories organize the 200 concepts in the Intellectual Property Management & Legal Technology learning graph.

## Categories

### FOUND - Foundation Concepts
Core intellectual property concepts and legal fundamentals that serve as prerequisites for all other topics. Includes IP types, basic legal principles, and foundational knowledge.

### PAT - Patent Prosecution
Concepts related to the patent application lifecycle including drafting, filing, examination, office actions, responses, and patent grant processes.

### TM - Trademark Management
Concepts covering trademark search, classification, application, registration, renewal, and international trademark systems.

### DOCK - Docketing Systems
Concepts related to IP docketing processes, deadline management, automated and manual docketing, patent office data feeds, and related systems.

### INTL - International IP
Concepts covering PCT applications, foreign filing, international patent and trademark systems, major patent offices, and harmonization efforts.

### IDS - Disclosure & Citations
Concepts related to Information Disclosure Statements, citation management, reference entry, prior art citations, and duty of candor requirements.

### QA - Quality Assurance
Concepts covering patent proofreading, OCR technology, bibliographic data verification, error detection, and quality control protocols.

### PORT - Portfolio Management
Concepts related to IP portfolio strategy, valuation, analytics, competitive intelligence, renewals, annuity payments, and portfolio maintenance.

### TECH - Legal Technology
Concepts covering IP management software platforms, system integration, data migration, workflow automation, and API connectivity.

### AI - AI & Machine Learning
Concepts related to artificial intelligence in IP management including machine learning, NLP, generative AI, automated tools, and AI ethics.

### OUTS - Outsourcing & Services
Concepts covering IP outsourcing evaluation, vendor management, service level agreements, cost analysis, and communication protocols.

### SEC - Security & Compliance
Concepts related to IP data security, cybersecurity, encryption, access control, data privacy regulations, professional responsibility, and ethics.

### OPS - Operations & Strategy
Concepts covering process optimization, change management, digital transformation, training, workforce development, and future trends.
