# Intellectual Property Management & Legal Technology

Welcome to the **Black Hills IP** intelligent textbook on Intellectual Property Management and Legal Technology.

This comprehensive resource covers the complete lifecycle of IP management, from patent prosecution and trademark registration through portfolio maintenance, renewals, and strategic decision-making. Special emphasis is placed on modern AI-powered tools and automation technologies transforming the IP legal services industry.

## What You Will Learn

- **IP Fundamentals** - Patents, trademarks, copyrights, and trade secrets
- **Patent Prosecution** - Application drafting, examination, and office action responses
- **Docketing Systems** - Automated deadline management and patent office integration
- **Trademark Management** - Search, registration, and international protocols
- **AI-Powered Tools** - Generative AI assistants, automated prosecution, and predictive analytics
- **Portfolio Strategy** - Valuation, analytics, and business-aligned IP management
- **Legal Technology** - Software platforms, workflow optimization, and digital transformation

## Who This Is For

This textbook is designed for IP professionals, paralegals, patent attorneys, corporate IP managers, law firm administrators, and legal technology specialists who want to understand and leverage modern IP management practices and tools.
