# Chapters

This textbook is organized into 13 chapters covering 200 concepts.

## Chapter Overview

1. [Intellectual Property Fundamentals](01-ip-fundamentals/index.md) - Introduces the core types of intellectual property, their legal foundations, professional responsibility, and ethical obligations.
2. [Patent Applications, Claims and Patentability](02-patent-applications-claims-patentability/index.md) - Covers patent types, applications, claims drafting, prior art analysis, and patentability criteria.
3. [Patent Prosecution Through Grant](03-patent-prosecution-through-grant/index.md) - The complete USPTO prosecution lifecycle from filing through examination, office actions, appeals, and patent grant.
4. [IP Docketing and Deadline Management](04-ip-docketing-deadline-management/index.md) - Docketing systems, automated and manual processes, deadline management, and electronic filing systems.
5. [Trademark Registration and Management](05-trademark-registration-management/index.md) - Trademark search, classification, application, prosecution, registration, renewal, and Madrid Protocol.
6. [International Filing and PCT](06-international-filing-pct/index.md) - Priority dates, convention priority, Paris Convention, PCT procedures, and foreign filing requirements.
7. [Global Patent Offices and IP Treaties](07-global-patent-offices-ip-treaties/index.md) - EPO, JPO, CNIPA, KIPO, IP5 offices, WIPO, TRIPs Agreement, and regional patent systems.
8. [Information Disclosure and Patent Quality](08-information-disclosure-patent-quality/index.md) - IDS preparation, duty of candor, citation management, patent proofreading, and quality control.
9. [Patent Renewals and Portfolio Management](09-patent-renewals-portfolio-management/index.md) - Renewal management, annuity payments, portfolio valuation, landscape analysis, and strategy alignment.
10. [IP Management Software and Platforms](10-ip-management-software-platforms/index.md) - Major IP software platforms, evaluation, data migration, system integration, and workflow automation.
11. [AI and Machine Learning in IP](11-ai-machine-learning-in-ip/index.md) - AI foundations, machine learning, NLP, generative AI, LLMs, automated tools, and AI ethics.
12. [Ethics, Security and Professional Development](12-ethics-security-professional-development/index.md) - Data security, compliance, malpractice prevention, cloud solutions, and training programs.
13. [IP Outsourcing, Analytics and Operations](13-ip-outsourcing-analytics-operations/index.md) - Outsourcing, vendor management, analytics dashboards, process optimization, and future trends.

## How to Use This Textbook

Chapters are ordered to respect concept dependencies - each chapter builds on knowledge from previous chapters. Begin with Chapter 1 for foundational concepts, then proceed sequentially. Chapters 4-8 cover specialized domains that can be studied in the order presented. Chapters 10-13 cover technology, AI, and operations topics that integrate knowledge from earlier chapters.

---

**Note:** Each chapter includes a list of concepts covered. Make sure to complete prerequisites before moving to advanced chapters.
